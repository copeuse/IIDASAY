INTERNET IS DYING AND SO ARE YOU

was a webradio curated by névé, louisæ, score16, mathilda b, labtoxin, chmod777 and synette

track selection used to changes based on the hour, paris time. new tracks was added every day

INTERNET-IS-DYING-AND-SO-ARE-YOU.COM was an opensource webradio with a basic chat system, both built with very simple tools. It was an ode to the ephemeral, to the punk movement, and to early internet days. A webradio springs to life, lives, and dies. It's impossible to archive it. If no one listens to it, it no longer truly exists. It's a moment, a moment you could have experienced together, before it disappeared ...forever...? really....?

i also wanted to thanks all the devs that helped me with my messy project : n1ark, Lime68k, fleure jaune champetre and 0x6b617465
